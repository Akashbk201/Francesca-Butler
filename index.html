Rooted Pathways Behaviour Consultancy provides behaviour analysis and consultancy services, supporting individuals, families, and organisations to create meaningful, lasting change.

I provide assessments, interventions, and training for individuals and services, using evidence-based approaches like Positive Behaviour Support (PBS) and Organisational Behaviour Management (OBM).

I support individuals of all ages with a variety of needs, as well as organisations looking to improve staff practices, reduce restrictive practices, and enhance service quality.

I provide behaviour-based consultancy to improve staff training, reduce restrictive practices, implement Positive Behaviour Support, and enhance overall service quality. My work also supports organisations in meeting regulatory standards, including preparing for Ofsted and CQC inspections by embedding best practices that promote positive outcomes for both staff and the individuals they support.

I work with care services, schools, residential settings, supported living providers, and other organisations that support individuals with additional needs.
